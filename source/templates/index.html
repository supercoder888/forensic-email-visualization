Analysing E-mail mailboxes using Visualization

Welcome,

this tool is developed during a Bachelor Thesis in 2015 at the Chair of Computer Science 1 of the Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen-Nuernberg. It allows the forensic examiners the investigate serveral mailbox files (including MBOX, PST and OST) at one time and to extract interesting patterns and clues of a conversation.

To offer you a productive environment the tool is separated into two pandels:

  • The left one displays several charts and diagramm in order to give you a quick overview of the number of E-mail in those accounts. It includes bar, row, and pie charts.
  • On the right the centerpiece of the tool is provided by spanning a forced graph to visualize the whole communication of those accounts. With the help of that graph you are able to explore the present communication

By clicking on the "Start" button you are directed to the investigation panel and you are able to begin with the analysing.
